About FrED Factory

A strategic research & educational initiative at Tec de Monterrey, in collaboration with MIT

Inter-Generational Learning

Senior students mentor and train junior cohorts, accelerating knowledge transfer and creating a self-sustaining research community rooted in hands-on smart manufacturing education.

Industry 4.0/5.0

Students tackle real industrial challenges aligned with the current and future needs of smart manufacturing, from AI to digital twin development and computer vision.

Multi-Regional Collaboration

Active in both MTY and CCM campuses, fostering a strong academic network bridging undergraduate, graduate and postgraduate researchers alongside industry partners.

Research with Impact

25 published papers in Q1/Q2 journals, 7 under review, 1 book chapter, and secured funding with the US Navy, demonstrating meaningful academic and industrial impact.
