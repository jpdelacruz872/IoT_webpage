About FrED Factory

FrED Factory is a digital platform that connects different academic and technical resources in one official website. The main purpose of the page is to organize access to tools, project information, and service sections in a clear way.

This platform is intended to integrate mini web pages for different functions instead of developing every tool directly inside the main page. In this way, users can move from the official page to specific sections such as recent papers, workshop calendar, virtual factory simulator, and inventory resources.

The idea is to make the website easier to use, visually organized, and functional for students and project members who need fast access to the available tools and information.
